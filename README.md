# Ferns and Petals Sales Analysis Dashboard

## Project Overview

This project analyzes the sales dataset of **Ferns and Petals (FNP)**, a gifting company specializing in products for occasions such as Diwali, Raksha Bandhan, Holi, Valentine’s Day, Birthdays, and Anniversaries.

The objective of this project is to uncover business insights related to:

* Sales trends
* Customer behavior
* Product performance
* Delivery efficiency
* Occasion-based purchasing patterns

The dashboard helps stakeholders make data-driven decisions to improve sales strategies and customer satisfaction.

## Dashboard Features

### 1. Total Revenue KPI

Displays the overall revenue generated from all orders.

### 2. Average Delivery Time

Shows the average time taken between order placement and delivery.

### 3. Monthly Sales Trend

Visualizes monthly revenue fluctuations across 2023.

### 4. Top Revenue-Generating Products

Identifies products contributing the highest revenue.

### 5. Customer Spending Insights

Analyzes average customer spending patterns.

### 6. Top 5 Product Performance

Tracks sales performance of the top-performing products.

### 7. Top Ordering Cities

Highlights the top 10 cities with the highest number of orders.

### 8. Quantity vs Delivery Analysis

Examines whether larger order quantities affect delivery time.

### 9. Occasion-Based Revenue Analysis

Compares revenue generated during different gifting occasions.

### 10. Occasion-Wise Product Popularity

Shows which products are most popular for specific occasions.
